## Product Rule

This product models a company as an AI-operated organization. Agents are not human employees; they are executable skill assets that can be assigned to roles, departments, projects, workflows, and incentive systems.

## Design Rule

Favor dense, operational, enterprise-grade UI. Avoid marketing-style hero screens. The first screen should help operators inspect company strategy, organizational structure, active agents, project allocation, finance/token status, and risk signals.

## Safety Rule

Financial and token mechanisms in this example are product-management models, not legal, accounting, investment, payroll, securities, or tax advice. Real-world deployment needs professional review.
